Professional Experience

Dr. David Mahalak is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Applied Logistics Integration (ALI) Consulting, an award-winning strategic business management firm that specializes in data analysis/management, decision analysis, lean six sigma, process improvement, software development, and supply chain management. Dr. Mahalak received his Doctorate of Engineering in Engineering Management from The George Washington University, and his doctoral research developed an easy-to-use Decision Support System (DSS) that applied advanced mathematical algorithms to analyze trade-offs among alternatives for Department of Defense (DoD) acquisition programs. Over his 10+ years of professional experience, Dr. Mahalak has employed advanced data analytics to help businesses and organizations efficiently and effectively manage over $35B in assets and realize significant financial savings as well as increased utilizations of resources through customized solutions.
